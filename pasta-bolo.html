Pasta bolognese

  • Macaroni
  • Lasagne
  • Pasta bolognese

Ingredienten

  • 250g macaroni (or any pasta of your choice)
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • 1 ½ cups of grated cheese (cheddar works well)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: breadcrumbs for topping

Bereiding

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions until al dente.
  3. Drain and set aside.
  4. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  5. Stir in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly (this is called a roux).
  6. Slowly whisk in the milk, making sure there are no lumps.
  7. Continue cooking and stirring until the mixture thickens.
  8. Reduce the heat and stir in the grated cheese until it’s melted and smooth.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
